# Background Source Policy

Ayah Studio uses free background videos only.

Approved sources:

- Pexels: primary source for Makkah, Madinah, mosques, and nature.
- Pixabay: backup source for Makkah, Madinah, nature, sea, and sky.
- Mixkit Stock Video Free License: backup source for nature, sea, and sky only.

Do not import or use:

- Pro or Premium clips.
- Paid stock sources.
- Shutterstock, iStock, or Adobe Stock results.
- Restricted clips.
- Editorial-only clips.
- Clips with visible watermarks or source logos.

Every background entry must be marked:

- `licenseScope: "free-commercial"`
- `licenseName`
- `licenseUrl`
- `sourceUrl`
- `downloadUrl`
